About Priya

My Story

I’m always curious to carry out an in-depth study on a topic that interests me. I enjoy the ingenuity in the use of technical resources to make innovative work. I am continually improving myself and striving to keep things orderly, whether in the workplace or in my personal life.
Design was just one of my hobbies, which kept growing to the extent that I wanted to turn it into a full-time career. Since then, I’ve been continuously searching for online resources and tutorials for improvement, but my journey as a designer began when I worked on a game application project as a tester. Though I was primarily responsible for checking quality control of the software , I had an opportunity to be involved in user research and design phases. That journey had taught me how user-centric designs are crucial to the success of the product. That’s when I was interested to learn more about user experience and interface design.
