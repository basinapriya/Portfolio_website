Objective

Design to develop a responsive web app for Vacation home rentals that satisfies the needs of its customers and fixes the challenges they face with the existing applications.

Overview

The product is an online platform that connects people who want to rent their homes with people who are looking for accommodations in that locale. It allows consumers to locate travel and holiday rentals within their budget and to make a reservation. Guests searching for exclusive listings and adventure all over the world can find what they want on this platform.
